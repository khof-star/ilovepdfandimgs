Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At ilovepdf&img.com, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our tools to convert, compress, or manage PDF and image files.

2. What We Collect

  • Uploaded Files: Temporarily stored for processing and deleted within 2 hours.
  • Cookies: Used only for analytics and user preferences. No tracking cookies.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We track tool usage patterns to improve service quality. No personal data is linked.

3. File Security

All file transfers are encrypted via HTTPS. Files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 2 hours of upload completion.

4. No Account Required

You can use all features without creating an account. We do not collect names, emails, or passwords.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage. No personal data is shared or sold.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal information (if any) by contacting us at support@ilovepdfandimg.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
